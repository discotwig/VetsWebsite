Vietnam Veterans Recognition

The Georgia Department of Veterans Services along with the Department of defense are participating in a joint commemoration effort to recognize the service of Vietnam veterans. Vietnam veterans are now eligible for Georgia's Vietnam War Certificate of Honor. Families are also eligible as well. Reach out to the GDVS now to recieve your certificate!

Volunteers Needed For The 2019 Christmas Chapter Dinner

We are once again very excited to be hosting another holiday dinner for all of our chapter members, friends, and family. As the holiday comes closer, please feel free to spread the word and invite as many people as you want. In addition to this, we are also looking for five to ten volunteers to help setup food stations and tables. Let us know if you are available!

Holiday Canned Food Drive

Members, We have begun a canned food drive for the holiday season. If you have any canned food, soups, vegetables, etc that you are not using or would like to donate, please bring them to our next chapter meeting and place them in the donation box.
